Projects

“For the things we have to learn before we can do them, we learn by doing them.”
- Aristotle

One of the hardest parts of doing projects whether software, hardware or otherwise, is facing the fear of failing or not "knowing enough". The truth is you will fail alot, I cannot emphasize how much you will suck. However as you go down the rabbit hole you will grow and learn more than you ever could from books or all the blogs in the world. Do not be afraid to fail, do not worry about being imperfect because in all honesty perfection is impractical. Practice does not make perfect, practice makes better.
